1. Italy

State in the south of Europe. It borders Switzerland and Austria to the north, Slovenia to the east, and France to the northwest. It is bordered on the east by the Adriatic Sea, on the south by the Ionian and Mediterranean Seas, and on the west by the Tyrrhenian, Ligurian, and Mediterranean Seas. Italy owns the islands of Elba, Sicily, Sardinia and several small islands.

2. France

If it weren't for France, there probably wouldn't be much beauty in the world. The magnificent landscapes of Provence inspired artists to their best paintings, slim and slender Parisian women brought fashion into a cult and showed everyone what true chic and elegance is, and France has given the world many famous writers and architects. And if not for Paris, it would not be the most beautiful and romantic capital for lovers. That is why it is impossible to imagine our planet without France. Probably most people at least once in their lives had an irresistible desire to visit this fabulous country, and rightly so. A country of wonderful music, delicious cheese, perfect wine and intoxicating aromas.

3. Poland

It seems that Poland is somewhere very close, and that life there is somewhat reminiscent of ours. However, this opinion seems to be wrong, and to be sure, you need to urgently go to Poland! In Poland, you will be surprised by a completely different architecture, a truly European way of life, unique traditions and cuisine. All of the above will pleasantly surprise you and make you return to this country again and again. The resort regions of the country are divided into two parts: in the north - beaches and the sea, in the south - healing and mineral springs and ski slopes. Somewhere in the middle are the sights of Warsaw, Teutonic castles, ancient Krakow, Poznan and Lodz, the Masurian Lakes. All these places can be explored endlessly.
